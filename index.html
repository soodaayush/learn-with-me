Who am I?

I am a tutor. I am a person that loves to teach children and guide them towards their dreams. I always loved children's creativity, what they think, and their opinions on certain topics. I have been teaching for a long time and will continue to teach for the rest of my life.

Why me?

My teaching space is a space for students to socialize, collaborate, become friends, etc. I have 15 years of experience teaching children. I love teaching kids and making them understand what's wrong and what's right. If you want me to teach your kids I will make sure I will guide them to success.

What I offer

  • A Different Way of Learning

    Your children will learn in ways that barely any other teachers teach. Your children will learn about how to be productive, how to take notes effectively, etc.

  • Preparing your kids for the future

    Your children will be prepared for the real world. They will know how to get basic needs, how to be financially secure, how to pay bills, etc.

  • A Welcoming Environment

    Your children will learn in a environment where they feel welcomed, calm, and organized.

Who is this for?

This is for kids aged four to ten. They will be learning things based on their age/grade, so that everything is appropriate for everyone.

What I teach

  • Art

    I teach your children about the basics of art, drawing, painting, dabbing, etc. They will get to know very creative artists who made great art. They will learn how to paint, how to use pastels, how to be creative, etc.

  • Crafting

    I teach your children about crafting. I teach them woodworking, how to use machinery, etc. They will get to see the amazing things people have crafted.

  • Summer Projects

    I usually do special projects during the summer because it's the best time of the year.
